About us

Welcome to Maruhiti Tea Shop, a cozy tea shop that started with a simple love for tea and traditional Newari dishes. What began as a humble venture by a husband and wife, offering local tea alongside popular snacks like bara and aalu chop, has now blossomed into a place where tea lovers can explore a wide variety of hot and cold teas, paired with an assortment of delicious snacks.
At Maruhiti Tea Shop, we are dedicated to providing a warm and welcoming atmosphere where every sip of tea feels like home. Our commitment to quality, authenticity, and customer satisfaction is what sets us apart. Whether you're stopping by for a refreshing iced tea or savoring a cup of hot tea with your favorite snack, we strive to make every visit a delightful experience.
Come join us at Maruhiti Tea Shop, where tradition meets innovation, and every cup tells a story!
